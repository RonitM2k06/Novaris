# Novaris v1.0.0

Initial public release of Novaris, a graph-based macroeconomic digital twin for causal shock simulation and historical replay.

## Highlights

* Causal Economic Knowledge Graph
* Historical Crisis Replay Engine
* Data Calibration Pipeline
* Nonlinear Shock Propagation
* Sticky Price & Adaptive Agent Modeling
* Explainability & Causal Trace Generation
* FastAPI Simulation Backend
* Next.js Command Center Interface

## Validation

The platform was evaluated against multiple historical macroeconomic events, including:

* 2008 Financial Crisis
* COVID-19 Economic Shock
* 2022 Energy Crisis
* Dot-Com Crash
* 1970s Oil Embargo

## Research Focus

Novaris explores graph-based causal simulation as an alternative to traditional forecasting approaches, emphasizing explainability, scenario analysis, and systemic ripple-effect modeling.
